My favorite Music Videos of ROSÉ

Roseanne Park is a New Zealand and South Korean songwriter and singer.
She debuted as a member of Blackpink in 2016.

All music videos are from the official YouTube account of ROSÉ roses_are_rosie

Video 1: Number One Girl

From the song title, I kind of guess what's the content and I was right on. I love the lyrics as a counter-lesson on the importance of loving yourself rather than living up to someone's expectations and losing your authenticity.

Video 2: toxic till the end

I love the pink hair of Evan Mock who was a pro skateboarder! This is the main reason I like this video! The other reason is that the fight and struggle in a relationship were softened and less violent. I could watch this with my teenage niece and start a meaningful conversation about dating and relationships.

Video 3: APT.

Even as an Asian American who is not into K-pop, I came across to this song in October 2024 before it got popular in the US radio stations in December. It was spinning in my head for several months which was powerful.
This song demonstarates the influence of K-pop culture in the West, beyond adding Korean words in the lyrics but introducing a popular drinking-counting game.
